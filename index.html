About the creator of this Restaurant

Hello! My name is Johnny Colonna, and I have been a picky eater for my entire life. I don't usually enjoy trying new foods becuase if I don't enjoy it then i feel as if I've wasted my money! I decided to create this restaurant for people like me that just want to get down to the basic and just enjoy some simple foods, at a fairly low price. As a certified picky eater, I can assure you that everything on the menu is as simple as it can get. And if you are unsure if there are certain ingredients, feel free to ask our expertly trained picky eater staff. We look forward to cooking some simple dishes for you!
